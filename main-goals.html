01. Exploit through AI

To create and exploit our Economic Big Data through computer and online AI techniques -our so called @I technology.

Our Economic Big Data and initial goal focuses on Spain at regional level, firstly, in order to facilitate access, visualization, exploitation and discovery of new patterns of economic and social behavior.

02. User Experience




To develop a friendly and appropriate graphic design for each type of variable using the most advanced programming techniques.

03. Reproducibility 



To elaborate the simple protocol followed for obtaining each variable from its original official source, with the objective that anyone can replicate the work and be able to both update the data and detect possible errors with ease.

04. Apply Knowledge


To exploit our Economic Big Data with the most advanced computational techniques, artificial intelligence or other type for various purposes and areas of knowledge -for example, economy, business plans, public policies, statistics, programming, graphic design and data visualization, etc. 

05. Accessibility

To contribute to the best, most accurate and accessible information for interested people, especially through the dissemination and use of this new database at the service of the mass media.
